Frequently Asked Questions

Questions from international members

Can I pay with an international credit card?
Yes. Visa, Mastercard, and American Express are accepted from any country. Pro is $5/month and Premium is $15/month. Payments are processed securely via Stripe. No hidden fees.
Is the content available in English?
Yes. All distillery profiles have English pages. The AI concierge KANADE responds in English on all plans. Our guide section covers imo shochu, mugi shochu, awamori, GI-protected regions, koji types, and drinking styles — all in publication-quality English.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Cancel anytime from your account settings. You will not be charged after cancellation. Your access continues until the end of the current billing period. No cancellation fees, no questions asked.
What is an AI credit?
One credit = one short AI response (up to ~120 characters of output). Longer answers consume 2–3 credits. Free plan: 5 credits/month. Pro: 100/month. Premium: 300/month. Credits reset on the 1st of each month. Premium members can purchase additional credits if needed.
I'm traveling to Kagoshima — which plan helps most?
Pro is ideal for travel planning. Ask AI KANADE "Which Kagoshima imo shochu distilleries offer tours?" or "Best distilleries to visit near Ibusuki?" Get personalized itinerary advice covering all GI-protected shochu regions: Satsuma, Kuma, Iki, and Ryukyu awamori.
Can I change or upgrade my plan later?
Yes. Upgrade any time and the change takes effect immediately. Downgrade takes effect at the next billing cycle. You can switch between plans freely from your account settings.
Is Terroir HUB SHOCHU affiliated with any distillery or spirits brand?
No. Terroir HUB SHOCHU is an independent database operated by FOMUS LLC (Japan). We are not affiliated with, sponsored by, or endorsed by any distillery or spirits brand. All data is sourced from public official sources only.
